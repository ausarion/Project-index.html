About us

Urban World Community Links (U.W.C.L.), the goal of this website is to create a One World Culture, through Links, links that will connect Urban Community Organizations (UCO) Worldwide. The objective is to integrate diverse cultures, from different part of the world, bringing awareness to the Social, Economic, Political and Environmental disparities in our society. Target Audience: Any Local, State Community Organizations, Nonprofit or Independent groups, entrepreneurs, artists or celebrities, who are active or inactive, physically or financially involved and contributing to making Social Economic, Political and Environmental Community changes in our society. My website will give anyone who shared common interest, the opportunity to not only voice their concerns, but post beneficial information and share what contributions they are making towards these changes.
