Welcome!

To Urban World Community Links (U.W.C.L.), the goal of this website is to create a One World Culture, through Links, links that will connect Urban Community Organizations (UCO) Worldwide. The objective is to integrate diverse cultures, from different parts of the world, bringing awareness to the Social, Economic, Political and Environmental disparities in our society.
